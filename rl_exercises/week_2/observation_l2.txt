If I don’t have access to transition probabilities, then I’m left only with V_before. 
And if I don’t have a reward signal, I also don’t know in which direction my goal 
lies or where I can gain more points. In other words, we may receive rewards, but 
we didn’t know in advance that we would get them or how to achieve them.
Regarding the transition function, instead of knowing the probabilities of ending 
up in state A or B, we only observe the state we actually land in and treat it as 
the ground truth. However, we have no information that we could have ended up in a different state as well.
It’s like a blind chicken that occasionally finds a grain by chance, but has no ability 
to anticipate where the next grain might be.
