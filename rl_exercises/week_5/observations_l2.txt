- How does the length of the trajectories affect the training?
=> It seems when the returns are low the trajectories are short. The agent is able to learn basic balancing mechanism due to the credit assignemnt being small.
As the agent gets better the trajectories also increases indicating high variance. The agent does perform poorly at the end or vise versa becoming noisy because of this high variance

=> No it will not work as, the learning rate will be incredibly high for policy gradient. On LunarLander this high of a learnig rate will cause the policy parameters to overshoot optimal regions in the policy space
causing the landing craft to get out of control.
